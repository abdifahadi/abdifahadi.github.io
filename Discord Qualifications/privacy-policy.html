Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Abdi Fahadi Discord Bot (“the Bot”, “we”, “our”, “us”) handles user data and protects privacy in accordance with Discord’s Developer Terms and Privacy Guidelines.

1. Information We Collect

The bot collects minimal and non-personal information required for its functionality, such as:

  • Server ID and name
  • User ID (for command tracking or prayer reminders)
  • Guild or user settings (e.g. timezone, prayer city, voice preferences)

No personal information such as names, emails, IP addresses, or messages is stored or processed.

2. How We Use Information

The data collected is used only to:

  • Provide personalized prayer time alerts and reminders.
  • Play nasheeds or Qur’an recitations upon request.
  • Improve the stability and reliability of the bot’s services.

3. Data Storage and Security

All stored data is kept securely in encrypted form (if applicable) and only used internally by the bot. We do not share, sell, or transmit any user data to third parties.

4. Data Deletion

Server administrators can request data deletion at any time by removing the bot from the server or contacting the developer directly. All related data will be automatically purged.

5. Third-Party Services

The bot may use third-party APIs such as Aladhan API for prayer times and YouTube for audio playback. These services have their own privacy policies and are governed by their respective providers.

6. Children's Privacy

This bot is not intended for users under the age of 13. By using the bot, you confirm that you meet Discord’s minimum age requirements.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the bot after any updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
